About Me

My name is Shoroqu Othman, my pronouns are she/her, and I am from Augusta, Georgia. I am a second year Literature, Media, & Communication Student at the Georgia Institute of Technology with Media and Design as my threads. I have an interest in Animation, Graphic Design, Game Design, and Writing. Currently I work for the Center for Teaching & Learning as a Student Assistant under Dr. Lauren Barbeau creating instructional Animations for various Georgia Tech.

Prior to my employment at the Center for Teaching & Learning, I worked as an intern at a start-up called Code that Down LLC. At Code That Down, I worked with the Blender API and learned how to 3D model and use CAD Program, with the intention of coding a tool to assist with the automation of 3D modelling.

I am proficient in Python, Java, the Adobe Creative Suite, Procreate, Canva, Vyond. I am a fast learner and am willing to learn anything in order to help complete a project.
